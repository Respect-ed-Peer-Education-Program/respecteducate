The Sex(Ed) Guide Book

What is affirmative consent? How does emergency contraception work? Are there laws that protect students from sexual harassment at school? The Sex(Ed) Guide Book has the answers to all of these questions and more! The guide book is a comprehensive digital resource covering a wide array of sexuality and consent topics, from STDs to healthy relationships to gender identity.

Information included in the guide covers 92% of National Sexuality Guidelines through grade 12 and 84% of Oregon state high school health and sexuality learning standards. You’ll find links throughout, directing you to reputable sources, educational videos, and supplemental media. Whether or not you have participated in any of our Sex(Ed) programming, the guide is an invaluable resource you can keep and refer back as you encounter new experiences as a young person.

The guide is free to download for all youth.
