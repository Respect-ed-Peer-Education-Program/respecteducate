DROP-IN PROGRAMMING

Drop-In Programming

Community Discussion Space

Drop by our Zoom meetings every second Saturday and last Wednesday of the month at 2-3pm to spend time chatting with your peers about various sexuality topics. We hope these discussions can provide a space to not only learn but also build community with other young people during the times of COVID.

Invite a friend, cozy up with your computer and a snack, and let us know how your quarantine has been going.

You can find our upcoming discussion times and topics listed on our events calendar



Workshops

At Sex(Ed) workshops, you’ll have the opportunity to learn more about specific sexuality topics you’re curious about. Organized and presented by peer educators, program directors, and youth leaders, workshops will be webinar-style, with plenty of opportunity for questions and discussion.



Workshop topics could include mandatory reporting, bystander intervention, restorative justice, Title IX rights, and more! You can find our upcoming workshop times and topics listed on our events calendar
