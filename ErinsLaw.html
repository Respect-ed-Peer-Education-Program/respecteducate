ERIN'S LAW

Erin's Law

The Respect(Ed) curriculum is designed to be compliant with Erin's Law, a piece of legislation named after Erin Merryn that requires elementary and secondary schools to teach sex abuse prevention every year in every grade. Erin's law has been adopted, to varying degrees, in most of the U.S.
