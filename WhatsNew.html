Feb. 3 2020

Respect(Ed) student leaders speak out against fear tactics and victim-blaming in guest presentation

Respect(Ed) student leaders Lane Mikkelsen, Sam Nystrom-Costales, and Ilka Sankari were featured in an article covering their work to fight abstinence-based sex ed in their high school. Mikkelsen, Nystrom-Costales, and Sankari are protesting the fear tactics, victim-blaming, and abstinence-only attitude propagated by Richard Wistocki, a guest speaker hired by the Oregon school district to present to students and parents on cybercrime and online harassment. They point out that Wistocki's talks were not only harmful but also directly violated state guidelines for sex education. The three presented their complaints to the district school board last month, and demanded that a formal apology be issued. Nystrom-Costales has also started an online petition, which already has more than 100 signatures.
