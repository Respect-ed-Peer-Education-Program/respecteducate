ASK A QUESTION

Ask A Question

We’re here to answer all of your sexuality and consent related queries! Whether you’re curious about birth control efficacy or Title IX rights, shoot us a question and we’ll get back to you as soon as we can. Be sure to also check out our frequently asked anonymous questions and answers page, and our blog, where we often post more detailed answers to popular consent and sexuality questions.

(Please keep in mind that Respect(Ed) staff are obligated by mandatory reporting law to report any information about the past or present abuse of a minor to appropriate authorities. Otherwise, anything you share with us will remain entirely confidential.)

If you need more immediate help, please call 911 or one of the following 24/7 hotlines:

800-656-4673 for sexual assault
866-331-9474 for dating abuse or text LOVEIS to 22522
800-273-8255 for suicide prevention
800-786-2929 for runaway and homeless youth
