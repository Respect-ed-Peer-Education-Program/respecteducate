Respect(Ed) At Your School

Are You A Student?

The Respect(Ed) curriculum is free for all students! If you’re interested in putting the peer education model into practice at your school, write to us at board@respectedpeereducation.org. We’ll work together to make our materials relevant to your school’s particular needs and help you get other students and staff on board.

Are You An Educator?

While our program is student-focused, we’re always happy to talk with health teachers, curriculum coordinators, and other educators looking to bring peer education into their classrooms. Do you want to add Respect(Ed) to your curriculum? Need help empowering student leaders to become peer educators? Shoot us an email at board@respectedpeereducation.org.
