Respect(Ed) At Your School

Are You A Student?

The Respect(Ed) curriculum is readily available for all students! If you’re interested in putting the peer education model into practice at your school, let us know. We’ll work together to make our materials relevant to your school’s particular needs and help you get other students and staff on board.



Are You An Educator?

While our program is student-oriented, we’re always open to working health teachers, curriculum coordinators, administrators, and other educators looking to bring peer-led education into their classrooms. Do you want to include the Respect(Ed) program as a part of your curriculum? Do you want help empowering student leaders to take an active role in their health education? Get in touch with us today.
