Respect(Ed) profiled as part of #MeToo project

Apr. 8 2019

Directors Jade Pfaefflin Bounds, Jesse Pearce, Maya Corral, and Zoë Pringle were interviewed about the Respect(Ed) Peer Education Program as part of a journalistic project around #MeToo being run out of the University of Oregon. Read about how peer educators put theory into practice and make a real difference on the ground in the classroom. The Reporting #MeToo series is part of the university’s larger Catalyst Journalism Project promoting journalism that focuses on both investigation and solutions.
