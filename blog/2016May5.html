Spotlight on Respect(Ed) co-founder Maya Corral

May 5 2016

Maya Corral founded Respect(Ed) Peer Education Program when she was just 16 years old. Then a sophomore in high school, she approached her school board about a solution to the issue sexual harassment and assault at her high school. She then sat down with fellow students and teachers to design the curriculum that would eventually become the basis for the Respect(Ed) program we know today.
