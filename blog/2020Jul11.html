Board presents on consent and gender inclusion at the Virtual Oregon Country Fair

Jul. 11 2020

This past Saturday, the Respect(Ed) board gave a webinar-style talk for Fair in the Clouds, a virtual event put on by the Oregon Country Fair in lieu of the beloved annual Oregon festival in light of the COVID-19 pandemic.

Directors Maya Corral, Jesse Pearce, and Jade Pfaefflin Bounds spoke and answered questions about what consent, sexual violence prevention, bystander intervention, and gender inclusion look like at the Oregon Country Fair and other festivals. The workshop, entitled “Consent, Sexual Violence Prevention, and Gender Inclusion” was offered as part of the Fair’s Stream of Diverse Consciousness, a series of workshops centered around social justice and action.

The live stream was recorded and will be made available to watch shortly for those who missed the event.
