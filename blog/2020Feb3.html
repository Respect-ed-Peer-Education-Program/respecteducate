Respect(Ed) student leaders speak out against fear tactics and victim-blaming in guest presentation

Feb. 3 2020

Respect(Ed) student leaders Lane Mikkelsen, Sam Nystrom-Costales, and Ilka Sankari were featured in an article covering their work to fight abstinence-based sex ed in their high school. Mikkelsen, Nystrom-Costales, and Sankari are protesting the fear tactics, victim-blaming, and abstinence-only attitude propagated by Richard Wistocki, a guest speaker hired by the Oregon school district to present to students and parents on cybercrime and online harassment.
