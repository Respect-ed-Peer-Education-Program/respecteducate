Announcing Sex(Ed): a peer-led online sex education program

Aug. 8 2020

We’re thrilled to announce Sex(Ed), our 12-week virtual consent and sexuality course designed to fill the gap for Oregon students negatively impacted by distance learning this fall.

We all know that sexual health, consent, and sexual violence prevention education are already oft-neglected and deprioritized subjects, even though they are paramount to the lifelong wellbeing of students. Because of this year's distance and hybrid learning plans, we're worried this important instruction will receive even less attention and engagement than usual. Respect(Ed) is here to ensure sex ed happens this year by offering students a forum outside of school to discuss these topics.

Sex(Ed) is open to all Oregon students ages of 13-17. Meeting weekly, participants will get the chance to discuss with and learn from their peers about topics such as safe sex, consent, healthy relationships, and sexual violence prevention.

Learn more about the program and how you can register or apply to be a peer educator.
